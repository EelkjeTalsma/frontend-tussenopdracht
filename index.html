Details

The Prelude to Chaos Collection is a collection of cosmetics in VALORANT. Its contents were initially available to be obtained when the collection was first released as a bundle in the Store. After this, only its weapon skins can be obtained whenever they become available in a player's daily Featured Store Offers. The full bundle cost is 8,700 VP, with the knife costing 4350 VP and the individual guns for 2,175 VP. The Prelude to Chaos collection is the only Exclusive Exclusive-Edition bundle to not have Player Cards.
